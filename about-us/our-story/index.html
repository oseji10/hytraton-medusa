About Us

Our Story

Medusa Equip prides itself on being a highly regarded provider of high-pressure and flow control equipment, including the design and manufacture of Hydraulic and Gas Power Units, Valve Testing, Hose Testing, Actuation Control Systems, Flushing Rigs and High-Pressure Fatigue/Cycle test systems.

In addition to it’s proprietary range of Air Driven Liquid Pump and Gas Booster equipment, Medusa Equip creates custom-designed and engineered solutions to cover a wide range of pressure testing applications.

Medusa Equip are experts in high-pressure fluid control with a global network of distributors and are proud to be the supplier of choice to the Oil & Gas, petrochemical, power generation, aerospace, automotive and defence.

Our History

Medusa Equip was founded as a family business in 1981 and predominately serves the global Oil & Gas and Energy markets with high pressure equipment.

We have a comprehensive network of sales partners in Europe, USA & Canada, Central & South America, Norway, Africa, Caspian, Middle East, Southeast Asia and Australia, supporting our customers with equipment sales and aftersales service.

In June 2018, Medusa Group acquired Medusa Equip. Medusa Group’s expertise and capabilities will bolster Medusa Equip’s expansion into new markets such as aerospace and defence as well as improving their existing supply chain.

After nearly four decades of serving customers with our trusted high-pressure equipment, Medusa Equip relocated and further extended its UK manufacturing plant into larger and more flexible facilities. The move increased their capacity for growth and triggered the development of core products, standard and engineered systems.

Accreditations

Medusa Equip has a standardised approach to risk management and operates an Integrated Management System, comprising accreditation for ISO9001, ISO14001 and ISO45001. A structured continuous improvement outlook is fundamental to Medusa Equips business.
