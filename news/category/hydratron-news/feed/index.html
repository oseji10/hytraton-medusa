]]> To our Valued Business Partners,

In the light of recent developments with respect to the COVID 19 global outbreak, Pryme Group and Medusa Eaquip Ltd are reaching out to all Suppliers and Customers to advise of where we stand and what we are doing.

We want to reassure you that we are closely monitoring the situation along with any potential impacts it may have on the work we do for you, our supply chain and our workforce. We are following Government advice and taking all possible precautions.

Our manufacturing personnel are fortunately all local to their workplace and consequently travelling is de facto limited. Non-essential external business meetings are now practically all cancelled. We have enhanced cleaning procedures and implemented preventive measures to limit close interaction of our employees.

We have contacted our suppliers and are working with them to ensure that critical material and services do not suffer disruptions.

If you would like more information about the actions, we are taking, please do not hesitate to contact us (zeina.melville@hydratron.com , Mrs Zeina Melville, Group Commercial Director and/or Findlay Beveridge, Medusa Eaquip Managing Director at Fin@@hydratron.com ).

Meanwhile, we confirm that we are working through a large order book but still have headroom. We envisage no problems with delivering our current orders and if you wish to mitigate for any risk of shortage or disruption, we will work with you to increase our production to meet your needs. Please do not hesitate to get in touch with your usual contact in our organisation to action such request and/or our Group Commercial Director (whose email is stated above).

Should your business have been impacted by the outbreak, then we would ask if you can inform us so that we can collaborate in resolving any problems that you foresee.

We thank you in advance for your co-operation and we will keep you informed of further developments.

Best regards,

Findlay Beveridge

Managing Director – Pryme Products

Medusa Eaquip Ltd

]]> The office had a great day on Monday hosting a Macmillan Coffee Morning

On Monday, the office staff had a sugar-filled morning to help raise money for the great cause that is Macmillan Cancer Support. This is not the first year in which the Medusa Eaquip’s team hosts a baking charity event. In fact, our bakers are now well-versed veterans who prepare amazing creations worthy of a Bake-Off challenge.

These occasions offer a double incentive. Not only co-workers can enjoy cakes and sweets while socialising, but they also provide help for a cause in which they strongly believe.

What is the World’s Biggest Coffee Morning?

The Macmillan World’s Biggest Coffee Morning is an event that started in 1991. The idea was for people to gather to share a coffee and, at the same time, donate the cost of one to Macmillan. There is an official date for the Coffee Morning event which this year was Friday 27 September. However, for those who want to participate, simply signing up on the Macmillan website to receive a free Coffee Kit.

Medusa Eaquip’s team had a fabulous time

With the contributions made by the office staff and the building neighbours, who were invited to this event, we raised £125 and counting!

Many thanks to everyone for the splendid result!

]]> Following the Pryme Group acquisition of Medusa Eaquip Ltd in June 2018, we would like to advise you of the latest steps towards our full integration within the group.

Pryme Group’s new £9.5M facility in North Shields, North East England, has created factory space for Medusa Eaquip core product and systems build capability.

From 24th May, Medusa Eaquip’s current facility in Broadheath, Altrincham will cease to manufacture product and during the immediate few weeks production will be transitioned entirely to Pryme Group Newcastle.

Medusa Eaquip’s existing Sales, Supply Chain, Engineering, Finance and General Management activities will remain in Broadheath but will be relocated to new offices nearby our current office.

  • From 24th May all Medusa Eaquip part supplier deliveries and Medusa Eaquip product collections will be continue to be coordinated by our Broadheath team but will require to be delivered/collected at – Pryme Group, Unit L5, Intersect 19, Tyne Tunnel Estate, North Shields, NE29 7UT
  • From 3rd June all phone and email communications with our office staff will remain as they are but we will be located at our new offices –
    Earle House, Atlantic St, Altrincham WA14 5DD **This will be our new postal address from 3rd June.

Whilst we do not anticipate any issues during the transition, your continued cooperation during this period is greatly appreciated.

The post Medusa Eaquip and Italcontrol Partnership Delivers Its First Co-Branded Test Bench appeared first on Medusa Eaquip.

]]> Medusa Eaquip Ltd, the UK manufacturer and global supplier of high-pressure testing equipment and its supply partner Italcontrol, an Italian manufacturer with over 20 years’ experience in the field of valve testing and calibration, supplied a dual vertical and horizontal valve test bench in a lead time of fewer than 3 months. This “hybrid” system is the first collaboration from the partnership.

Andy Quayle, Managing Director of UK based, Medusa Eaquip Ltd said: “The test bench is a very impressive bespoke system, engineered for a major global valve manufacturing and servicing company and capable of testing valves with reaction forces up to 100 & 260 Tonnes. Both teams worked tirelessly and seamlessly to deliver this outstanding piece of equipment, to the highest quality standard and in the shortest possible lead time. Our combined efforts showcase the future potential for this great partnership.”

Fabio Castiglioni, Managing Director of Italian based Italcontrol added: “We are proud of our advanced product development and research into new technologies that has enabled us to build a powerful reputation for solving problems. We believe every product needs its own test bench, with different requirements or special tests and customised solutions, the partnership with Medusa Eaquip is an exciting opportunity to build on this belief and add significantly to our core problem-solving strengths.”

In its 24 year history, Italcontrol has been hugely successful in designing and building valve test equipment, particularly those that are large or complex and is among the most advanced provider of its type in the world. Medusa Eaquip, which was founded more than 35 years ago is recognised globally for creating innovative high-quality products and solutions for pressure testing equipment in the oil and gas, petrochemical, power generation, aerospace, automotive and defence industries.

The partnership was formed to expand the product offering and market penetration from both businesses and to reach a broader and more diverse market through offering a significantly wider range of testing equipment, including equipment for cryogenic and actuator testing.

]]> Medusa Eaquip and Italcontrol have agreed on a supply partnership which will position Medusa Eaquip as the distributor of Italcontrol products in the UK. In addition, the alliance will see Hydraton control and power systems being marketed worldwide in conjunction with Italcontrol work-holding and clamping structures.

Italcontrol has an excellent capability in such builds, in particular for large or complex arrangements. These “hybrid” systems will be co-branded in order to leverage the reputation of both parties for providing exceptional quality and service.

In its 24 year history, Italcontrol has been hugely successful in producing competitively priced, large complex rig designs supported by a supply chain located within a 5-mile radius of their manufacturing site in Marnate, Northern Italy. The supply Partnership will enable the parties to reach a broader and more diverse market and offer a far greater range of equipment, including equipment for cryogenic and actuator testing.

Formed more than 35 years ago, Medusa Eaquip is widely recognised for creating innovative, high-quality products and solutions that provide performance assurance. The company supplies the oil and gas, petrochemical, power generation, aerospace, automotive and defence industries.
