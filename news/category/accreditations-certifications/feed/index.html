]]> Medusa Equip Ltd is pleased to confirm its accreditation with ISO 45001, the new global standard for Health and Safety Management

As a business which has been supplying High Pressure Liquid and Gas equipment globally for 40 years we have always had our employees and our customers Health & Safety as our #1 priority.

As part of Medusa Group one of our core values is “HSE in everything we do” and this migration from the old OHSAS 18001 to ISO 45001 within our Integrated Management System demonstrates our commitment to protecting health, and providing safe solutions as well as our drive towards continuous improvement.

We look forward to servicing your requirements with this value at the heart of our activities.
